Delivery Information

   Once an order is placed on our website, the processing time typically takes 7 business days. If the garment includes customization, such as adding logos, text, or unique designs, please note that this will require additional time to complete. The complexity of the design may influence the overall processing time. After processing, the shipping time depends on the delivery location. For domestic orders, delivery usually takes between 6 and 8 business days. For international shipments, delivery can take anywhere from 1 to 2 weeks, depending on the destination and the shipping method chosen. Shipping fees are calculated based on the selected delivery method. Standard shipping is often free for orders over 2000 rupees amount, while expedited shipping is available for an additional fee, providing faster delivery. International shipping is available, but please be aware that customs duties, taxes, or other import fees may apply depending on the country of delivery. These additional charges are the responsibility of the customer. Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email. This number allows you to track your shipment in real time and stay updated on its location. You can also visit our website to enter the tracking number for detailed shipping updates. We currently offer delivery to a wide range of countries. If your location is not listed, please feel free to contact our customer support team, and we will assist you with shipping options. While we aim for timely delivery, unforeseen circumstances such as high demand, holidays, or adverse weather conditions may cause delays. If there are any significant delays, we will notify you as soon as possible and provide an updated estimated delivery window.
